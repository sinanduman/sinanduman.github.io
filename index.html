Chaos: Arnold's Cat Map
Arnoldâ€™s cat map in recognition of Russian mathematician Vladimir I. Arnold, who discovered it using an image of a cat. It is a simple and elegant demonstration and illustration of some of the principles of chaos - namely, underlying order to an apparently random evolution of a system. An image (not necessarily a cat) is hit with a transformation that apparently randomizes the original organization of its pixels. However, if iterated enough times, as though by magic, the original image reappears.
